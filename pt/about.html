What is my story and why this journal exists

ACL reconstruction and meniscus repair changed everything in an instant. What followed was months of deliberate, repetitive, often frustrating work.

This journal exists to document that work honestly.

I’m 42 years old, Polish, and living in Brazil. I’ve been an athlete my entire life—competing in tennis since age 7 and later in Masters ITF competitions. Beyond the court, my life has been built around movement: kitesurfing, surfing, snowboarding, and golf.

The Turning Point

Five years after a partial tear, a simple movement—cleaning a table—caused a complex bucket-handle meniscus tear. My knee locked, and I needed emergency surgery. My doctor recommended doing both the meniscus repair and ACL reconstruction at once.

"This journal documents the physical struggle, the slow progress, the setbacks, the doubts, and the emotional reality of rebuilding."

It’s written day by day, week by week, to make this path clearer and less lonely for anyone going through it.

You’re not broken.

You’re rebuilding.
