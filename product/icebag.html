While high-tech machines are great for home, the real battle with swelling happens when you leave the house. I found that a simple blue ice bag was my most reliable companion.

Mastering the Art of Real Ice

Bulk is Better

If you use ice bags properly, your freezer will never keep up. I started buying 5kg bags of ice in bulk. It‚Äôs cheap, and having a massive supply means you never hesitate to start a fresh session.

The Restaurant Hack

When traveling or out for lunch, don't be shy. Every restaurant has an industrial ice machine. Just ask the waiter to fill your bag‚Äîthey almost always say yes, and it keeps your recovery on track during social events.

Add a Splash of Water

Pro tip: Fill your bag with ice, then add just a half-cup of cold water. This creates an "ice slurry" that eliminates air pockets and allows the bag to mold perfectly to the shape of your kneecap.

Air is the Enemy

After filling, squeeze the bag to push all the air out before screwing the cap on. Removing the air prevents the bag from acting like a balloon and ensures maximum contact with your skin.
