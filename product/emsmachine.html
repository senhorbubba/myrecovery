Post-Op Tips for Success

Re-Educate, Don't Just Pulse

Don't just sit there. When the machine pulses, try to voluntarily contract your muscle along with it. This helps the brain-to-muscle connection recover much faster.

Pad Placement is Key

Being off by an inch matters. Use the PowerDot app's visual guides to find the "motor point" of your quad. If you don't see a clear contraction, move the pad slightly.

Keep Your Skin Clean

The biggest "hidden cost" is replacement pads. Clean your skin with an alcohol wipe before use; your pads will last 2x longer and the connection will feel much smoother.

Traveling? Charge First!

The PowerDot pods have a decent battery life, but they take a while to charge. Always top them off before a long flight so you can use the "Active Recovery" program mid-air.
