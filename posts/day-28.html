Week 4 Dec 14, 2025

Day 28: Rest, Rhythm, and Holding the Line

"A well-rested day that leaned into balance rather than pushing. Light movement, social time, and family rest kept things steady. The knee reacted with some swelling, but nothing unexpected or unmanageable."

Pain Level 2/10
Swelling 4/10

I woke up feeling well rested today. That alone set a different tone. I did my standard morning exercises, nothing intense and nothing forced. With physiotherapy happening every day, weekends now feel like active rest days. I still need to work on mobility, but without chasing volume or intensity.

Flexion felt slightly better, which was encouraging. Not a breakthrough, but enough to notice. Small signals like that matter more than big jumps right now.

I went out for brunch with friends. Some walking, a lot of sitting, and just being out for a while. As expected, the knee swelled again, but it stayed manageable. No sharp warnings, just the usual reminder that it still reacts to load and time.

Back home, I rested with the kids and watched a movie. Nothing productive on paper, but exactly what the day needed. Recovery also happens when nothing is being forced.

Key Takeaways

  • Prioritizing rest on weekends helps maintain rhythm
  • Light mobility work remains essential, even on rest days
  • Flexion shows small but positive signs of improvement
  • Social time and light activity still trigger some swelling
  • Rest and family time support recovery as much as exercise
